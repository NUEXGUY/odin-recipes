Mediterranean Salad

The Mediterranean salad is a celebration of fresh, wholesome ingredients that are both flavorful and nourishing. Drawing inspiration from the sunny Mediterranean coast, this salad combines crisp vegetables with protein-rich chickpeas, tangy olives, and creamy feta cheese. Each bite is a fusion of textures and tastes, making it a perfect dish to enjoy on a warm day or as a refreshing side to your main course.

Rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, this salad isn't just tasty but also incredibly healthy. The combination of ingredients is known to support heart health and provide an energy boost. Tossed in a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing, it captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine in every bite. Let's dive into the recipe and embark on a culinary journey to the Mediterranean!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (preferably red or yellow)
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (canned or boiled)
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, and chickpeas.
  2. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  4. Add the Kalamata olives and feta cheese to the salad and give it another gentle toss.
  5. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Let the salad sit for about 10 minutes to let the flavors meld, then serve and enjoy!
