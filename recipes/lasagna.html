Lasagna

This Lasagna is going to be the tastiest thing you've made all year!

Lasagna, a culinary icon of Italian cuisine, is renowned worldwide for its delicious and hearty characteristics. Layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, molten cheese, and perfectly cooked pasta, this dish offers a symphony of flavors and textures in every bite. Whether you're hosting a family dinner or simply craving a comforting meal, this lasagna recipe will surely transport you to the sun-drenched terrains of Italy.

Requiring minimal effort for a dish of such grandeur, our lasagna recipe promises not just a feast for your palate but also a delightful experience in the kitchen. The key lies in the balance of ingredients, ensuring every layer complements the other. Ready to dive into this culinary adventure? Gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and let's get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, sauté onions until translucent. Add the garlic and ground beef, cooking until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and season with salt and pepper. Allow the sauce to simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese, egg, half of the mozzarella cheese, half of the Parmesan cheese, and basil. Mix until well combined.
  5. Spread a thin layer of béchamel sauce at the bottom of a baking dish. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top.
  6. Layer with a third of the meat sauce, followed by a third of the cheese mixture. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil and enjoy!
